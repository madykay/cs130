Who I Am

I am a non-equity stage manager excited to help shape the current and future landscape of theatre into a more inclusive, supportive, and transformative environment for everyone involved. I am passionate about facilitating open and honest communication between artists and production staff alike in order to bring out the best in my collaborators.

Chicago-Based

As a recent alumna of Northwestern University, I have spent the past few years working in and around the Chicago theatre scene, and am excited to continue to work with all of the passionate artists this city has to offer.

Multi-Disciplinary

Beyond Stage Management, I also have extensive experience in the realm of Technical Direction and Scenic Design, as well as Production and Company Management.

TYA-Focused

I am passionate about Theatre for Young Audiences and the impact that art can have on children of any age.

Chess Player

Outside of theatre, I am also an accomplished chess player, with a USCF rating of 1050 and top prize in my section at multiple open tournaments.
